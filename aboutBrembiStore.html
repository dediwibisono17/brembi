About us

Brembi Store is an online shop that provide services for Line@ User in Indonesia. We know that many Line@ users in Indonesia have a problem to upgrade their line@ account. The boundaries for purchasing line@ Premium ID or monthly plan make a lot of Line@ user not use their line@ account in an ineffective way. Since LINE only provide the payment using credit card, we try to help users to upgrade their Line@ to become premium.

Started in 2015 from a FJB Kaskus (www.bitly/jasalineat) and Instagram Account (@BrembiStore) , Brembi Store already help more than 2000 Line@ Account to become Premium ID. From small online shop seller, Big Business Store, Startups, Local or School Communities, and Line@ Blogger already use our services. We provide the best customer service to help advance or newbie Line@ user to use Line@ Account.
